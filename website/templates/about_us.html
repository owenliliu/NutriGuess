Welcome to NUTRIGUESS

You will be presented with a mystery food and you have unlimited tries to guess what the food is. To make a guess, type your answer into the search bar and select it from the dropdown menu of foods. If your guess matches the mystery food, you win the game. If your guess does not match the mystery food, we will provide you with hints in the form of a table of nutrition facts for the selected food.

Welcome to Nutriguess, a wordle-type game that aims to educate people on making better nutritional choices by testing their knowledge on nutrition facts. Our game is designed to be fun and engaging, while also providing valuable information about the nutritional content of various foods.

Our team of nutrition experts has carefully researched and curated a list of foods and their nutritional facts to ensure that the game is both accurate and informative. We believe that by making nutrition education fun and interactive, we can help people make better food choices and improve their overall health and well-being.

At NutriGuess, we are committed to promoting healthy eating habits and providing valuable nutrition education to everyone. Whether you are a health enthusiast or just looking to learn more about nutrition, we hope that our game will be both entertaining and educational.
